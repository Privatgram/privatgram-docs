# PRIVATGRAM® Whitepaper

**Secure Enterprise Messaging Platform for Government, Banking and Critical Information Infrastructure**

Version 1.0

---

# Executive Summary

PRIVATGRAM® is a secure enterprise messaging platform developed for organizations that require confidential communications, centralized administration, and complete control over their communication infrastructure.

Unlike public messaging applications, PRIVATGRAM is designed for deployment inside enterprise environments where information security, operational governance, regulatory compliance and infrastructure ownership are strategic requirements.

The platform combines modern end-to-end cryptographic protocols with enterprise administration, enabling organizations to manage users, devices and communication policies while preserving the confidentiality of protected communications.

This whitepaper provides a high-level overview of the platform architecture, security model, deployment philosophy and future development roadmap.

---

# Vision

The increasing digitalization of government institutions, financial organizations and operators of critical infrastructure requires communication systems that are fundamentally different from consumer messaging platforms.

Organizations require solutions that provide:

- private deployment;
- centralized administration;
- secure communications;
- regulatory compliance;
- operational control;
- scalability.

PRIVATGRAM was created to address these requirements while maintaining usability comparable to modern enterprise collaboration platforms.

# Architectural Principles

The architecture of PRIVATGRAM is based on several core principles.

## Security by Design

Security is integrated into every architectural layer rather than introduced as an additional feature.

---

## Enterprise First

The platform is designed for organizational governance rather than consumer social networking.

---

## Modular Architecture

Major functional components are logically separated to improve maintainability and scalability.

---

## Cross-Platform Compatibility

A consistent user experience is provided across desktop and mobile environments.

---

## Private Infrastructure

Organizations retain ownership of their deployment and operational environment.

# Security Model

PRIVATGRAM implements a layered security architecture.

Primary security mechanisms include:

- End-to-End Encryption
- X3DH Key Agreement
- Double Ratchet
- AES-256-GCM
- HKDF
- Curve25519
- TLS 1.3

Security controls operate across multiple layers including devices, transport, enterprise administration and deployment infrastructure.

---

# Enterprise Administration

The platform provides centralized management capabilities.

Administrative functions include:

- role management;
